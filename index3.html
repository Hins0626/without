How much do you know about illnesses? Can all illnesses be cured only by taking medicine? What do we do in emergencies when we suddenly can't find any medicine?



Trust me, the content on this website can be helpful in certain situations.



I don't know how doctors in your area prescribe medications. In some places, they only prescribe medication when the illness has reached a certain severity. Before that, you have to endure a lot of pain on your own.



Because in the early stages of an illness, or when the illness is mild, we can return to normal health through gentler methods such as massage and bathing. As the illness progresses, more stimulating external treatment methods may be needed, and needles might be used. In these mild stages, it's possible to recover without taking internal medications, relying on external treatment methods to restore health.



As the illness progresses, the effectiveness of these methods diminishes, and we resort to internal medications for optimal results. More severe cases may even require intravenous therapy, and the effects of these medications are naturally stronger than physical therapies. However, do you know any doctors or their children who haven't taken a single pill from birth to high school due to illness?



It's either those children are exceptionally healthy and never get sick, or through certain methods, the illness is stopped at its early stage or cured directly.



It's truly incredible. Some doctors are willing to share their experiences, and I've gathered these experiences and put them on this website. While we can't expect to rely on these tips to never be troubled by illnesses again, it's hard to understand as much as these doctors do and avoid every illness. Still, it can help with some minor health issues, and I believe you might find them useful.
